# Verify Your Domain on DigitalOcean

This guide walks you through adding DNS records for Lettr on DigitalOcean's DNS management.

<Info>
  Make sure your domain is using DigitalOcean's nameservers before proceeding. You can verify this in your domain registrar's settings.
</Info>

## Prerequisites

- A domain added to your DigitalOcean account
- Access to the DigitalOcean Control Panel
- A Lettr account with your domain added

## Step 1: Add Domain to Lettr

1. Log in to your [Lettr dashboard](https://app.lettr.com)
2. Navigate to **Domains**
3. Click **Add Domain**
4. Enter your domain name and select the domain type
5. Copy the DNS records provided

## Step 2: Access DigitalOcean DNS

1. Log in to your [DigitalOcean Control Panel](https://cloud.digitalocean.com)
2. Click **Networking** in the left sidebar
3. Select the **Domains** tab
4. Click on your domain name

## Step 3: Sending Domain Setup

Choose the setup that matches your needs:

### Option A: Subdomain (Recommended)

Use a subdomain like `sender.example.com` for sending emails.

#### Bounce Domain CNAME

1. Click **Add Record** and select **CNAME**
2. Enter `sender` in the **Hostname** field (or your chosen subdomain)
3. Enter `sparkpostmail.com.` in the **Will Direct To** field (note the trailing period)
4. Set TTL to `3600` (or leave default)
5. Click **Create Record**

| DigitalOcean | Lettr | Example Value |
|--------------|-------|---------------|
| Hostname | Host | `sender` |
| Will Direct To | Value | `sparkpostmail.com.` |
| TTL | - | `3600` |

### Option B: Primary Domain

Use your root domain with TXT SPF verification.

#### SPF Record

1. Click **Add Record** and select **TXT**
2. Enter `@` in the **Hostname** field
3. Enter `v=spf1 include:_spf.sparkpostmail.com ~all` in the **Value** field
4. Click **Create Record**

| DigitalOcean | Lettr | Example Value |
|--------------|-------|---------------|
| Hostname | Host | `@` |
| Value | Value | `v=spf1 include:_spf.sparkpostmail.com ~all` |

<Warning>
  If you already have an SPF record, merge it by adding `include:_spf.sparkpostmail.com` before the `~all` or `-all` mechanism.
</Warning>

### DKIM Record (Required for both options)

1. Click **Add Record** and select **TXT**
2. Enter the DKIM hostname from your Lettr dashboard (e.g., `scph0226._domainkey.sender`)
3. Enter the DKIM value from your dashboard
4. Click **Create Record**

| DigitalOcean | Lettr | Example Value |
|--------------|-------|---------------|
| Hostname | Host | `scph0226._domainkey.sender` |
| Value | Value | `v=DKIM1;k=rsa;h=sha256;p=MIGf...` |

### DMARC Record (Required for both options)

1. Click **Add Record** and select **TXT**
2. Enter `_dmarc.sender` (subdomain) or `_dmarc` (root domain) in the **Hostname** field
3. Enter `v=DMARC1;p=none;` in the **Value** field
4. Click **Create Record**

| DigitalOcean | Lettr | Example Value |
|--------------|-------|---------------|
| Hostname | Host | `_dmarc.sender` or `_dmarc` |
| Value | Value | `v=DMARC1;p=none;` |

## Step 4: Tracking Domain Setup

Custom tracking domains require two CNAME records.

### SSL Certificate CNAME

1. Click **Add Record** and select **CNAME**
2. Enter the SSL verification hostname from your dashboard (e.g., `_acme-challenge.click.sender`)
3. Enter the SSL verification value from your dashboard
4. Click **Create Record**

### Traffic CNAME

1. Click **Add Record** and select **CNAME**
2. Enter your tracking subdomain (e.g., `click.sender`)
3. Enter `proxy.lettr-tracking.com.` (note the trailing period)
4. Click **Create Record**

| DigitalOcean | Lettr | Example Value |
|--------------|-------|---------------|
| Hostname | Host | `click.sender` |
| Will Direct To | Value | `proxy.lettr-tracking.com.` |

<Tip>
  SSL certificates are automatically generated and renewed by Lettr.
</Tip>

## Step 5: Inbound Domain Setup

To receive emails at your domain, add MX records.

1. Click **Add Record** and select **MX**
2. Enter `inbound` in the **Hostname** field (or your chosen subdomain)
3. Enter `rx1.sparkpostmail.com.` in the **Will Direct To** field
4. Set **Priority** to `10`
5. Click **Create Record**
6. Repeat for `rx2.sparkpostmail.com.` and `rx3.sparkpostmail.com.`

| DigitalOcean | Lettr | Priority |
|--------------|-------|----------|
| `inbound` | `rx1.sparkpostmail.com.` | 10 |
| `inbound` | `rx2.sparkpostmail.com.` | 10 |
| `inbound` | `rx3.sparkpostmail.com.` | 10 |

<Warning>
  Use a subdomain like `inbound.example.com` for receiving emails. Using your root domain will route ALL email to Lettr.
</Warning>

## Step 6: Complete Verification

1. Return to your [Lettr dashboard](https://app.lettr.com)
2. Navigate to **Domains**
3. Click **Verify** next to your domain
4. Wait for DNS propagation (usually 5-30 minutes for DigitalOcean)

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Records not showing in DNS checks">
    DigitalOcean DNS typically propagates quickly (5-10 minutes). If records aren't showing:
    - Ensure your domain is using DigitalOcean nameservers
    - Check for typos in record values
    - Try adding a trailing period to CNAME targets
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="CNAME conflicts">
    You cannot have a CNAME record at the root domain (`@`). Use the subdomain option or TXT SPF verification for root domains.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Multiple TXT records">
    DigitalOcean supports multiple TXT records on the same hostname. Add each record separately.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
