# Verify Your Domain on Linode

This guide walks you through adding DNS records for Lettr using Linode DNS Manager (now part of Akamai).

## Prerequisites

- A domain configured in Linode DNS Manager
- Access to your Linode Cloud Manager account
- A Lettr account with your domain added

## Step 1: Add Domain to Lettr

1. Log in to your [Lettr dashboard](https://app.lettr.com)
2. Go to **Domains**, open the tab for your domain type (e.g. **Sending Domains**), and click **+ New Sending Domain** (or the equivalent for Tracking, Inbound, or Storage)
3. Enter your domain name and copy the DNS records provided

## Step 2: Access Linode DNS Manager

1. Log in to the [Linode Cloud Manager](https://cloud.linode.com)
2. Click **Domains** in the left sidebar
3. Select your domain from the list
4. You'll see the DNS records management page

<Info>
  Make sure your domain's nameservers are pointing to Linode's nameservers (ns1.linode.com through ns5.linode.com).
</Info>

## Step 3: Sending Domain Setup

Choose the setup that matches your needs:

### Option A: Subdomain (Recommended)

Use a subdomain like `mail.example.com` for sending emails.

#### Bounce Domain CNAME

1. Click **Add a CNAME Record**
2. Enter `mail` in the **Hostname** field (or your chosen subdomain)
3. Enter `sparkpostmail.com` in the **Alias to** field
4. Set **TTL** to `Default` or your preferred value
5. Click **Save**

| Linode | Lettr | Example Value |
|--------|-------|---------------|
| Hostname | Host | `mail` |
| Alias to | Value | `sparkpostmail.com` |

### Option B: Primary Domain

Use your root domain with TXT SPF verification.

#### SPF Record

1. Click **Add a TXT Record**
2. Leave the **Hostname** field empty (for root domain)
3. Enter `v=spf1 include:_spf.sparkpostmail.com ~all` in the **Value** field
4. Click **Save**

| Linode | Lettr | Example Value |
|--------|-------|---------------|
| Hostname | Host | (empty) |
| Value | Value | `v=spf1 include:_spf.sparkpostmail.com ~all` |

<Warning>
  If you already have an SPF record, merge it by adding `include:_spf.sparkpostmail.com` before the `~all` or `-all` mechanism.
</Warning>

### DKIM Record (Required for both options)

1. Click **Add a TXT Record**
2. Enter the DKIM hostname from your Lettr dashboard (e.g., `scph0226._domainkey.mail`)
3. Enter the DKIM value from your dashboard in the **Value** field
4. Click **Save**

| Linode | Lettr | Example Value |
|--------|-------|---------------|
| Hostname | Host | `scph0226._domainkey.mail` |
| Value | Value | `v=DKIM1;k=rsa;h=sha256;p=MIGf...` |

### DMARC Record (Required for both options)

1. Click **Add a TXT Record**
2. Enter `_dmarc.mail` (subdomain) or `_dmarc` (root domain) in the **Hostname** field
3. Enter `v=DMARC1;p=none;` in the **Value** field
4. Click **Save**

| Linode | Lettr | Example Value |
|--------|-------|---------------|
| Hostname | Host | `_dmarc.mail` or `_dmarc` |
| Value | Value | `v=DMARC1;p=none;` |

## Step 4: Tracking Domain Setup

Custom tracking domains require two CNAME records.

### SSL Certificate CNAME

1. Click **Add a CNAME Record**
2. Enter the SSL verification hostname from your dashboard (e.g., `_acme-challenge.click.mail`)
3. Enter the SSL verification value from your dashboard in the **Alias to** field
4. Click **Save**

### Traffic CNAME

1. Click **Add a CNAME Record**
2. Enter your tracking subdomain (e.g., `click.mail`)
3. Enter `proxy.lettr-tracking.com` in the **Alias to** field
4. Click **Save**

| Linode | Lettr | Example Value |
|--------|-------|---------------|
| Hostname | Host | `click.mail` |
| Alias to | Value | `proxy.lettr-tracking.com` |

<Tip>
  SSL certificates are automatically generated and renewed by Lettr.
</Tip>

## Step 5: Inbound Domain Setup

To receive emails at your domain, add MX records.

1. Click **Add a MX Record**
2. Enter `inbound` in the **Hostname** field (or your chosen subdomain)
3. Enter `rx1.sparkpostmail.com` in the **Mail Server** field
4. Enter `10` for the **Preference**
5. Click **Save**
6. Repeat for `rx2.sparkpostmail.com` and `rx3.sparkpostmail.com`

| Linode Hostname | Preference | Mail Server |
|-----------------|------------|-------------|
| `inbound` | 10 | `rx1.sparkpostmail.com` |
| `inbound` | 10 | `rx2.sparkpostmail.com` |
| `inbound` | 10 | `rx3.sparkpostmail.com` |

<Warning>
  Use a subdomain like `inbound.example.com` for receiving emails. Using your root domain will route ALL email to Lettr.
</Warning>

## Step 6: Complete Verification

1. Return to your [Lettr dashboard](https://app.lettr.com)
2. Navigate to **Domains**
3. Click **Verify** next to your domain
4. Wait for DNS propagation (usually 5-30 minutes for Linode)

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="DNS records not propagating">
    Linode DNS typically propagates within 15-30 minutes. If records aren't showing:
    - Verify your domain uses Linode nameservers
    - Check for typos in record values
    - Use [WhatsMyDNS](https://www.whatsmydns.net/) to check propagation status
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="CNAME at root domain">
    Linode doesn't support CNAME records at the root domain. Use the subdomain option or TXT SPF verification for root domains.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Long TXT records">
    For long DKIM values, enter the complete value without line breaks. Linode handles long TXT records automatically.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
