## Quick Answers

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why isn't my email being delivered?">
    Check your domain verification status, ensure the recipient isn't suppressed, and verify you're not hitting rate limits. See [Delivery Issues](/knowledge-base/troubleshooting/delivery-issues) for detailed troubleshooting.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I verify my domain?">
    Add the DNS records shown in your dashboard, wait for propagation (15 min to 48 hours), then click Verify. See our [Domain Verification guides](/knowledge-base/domain-verification/introduction) for step-by-step instructions for your DNS provider.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What's the API rate limit?">
    Free: 10 req/sec, Pro: 100 req/sec, Enterprise: Custom. See [Rate Limits](/knowledge-base/troubleshooting/rate-limits).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I track email opens?">
    Open tracking is enabled by default. View stats in the dashboard or via the API. See [Analytics](/learn/sending/analytics).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
